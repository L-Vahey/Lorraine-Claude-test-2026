I'm Lorraine — a product designer based in Dublin with 7 years of experience shipping complex B2B products in sports, health, and data.

I've led design for products used by NFL medical staff, Premier League performance teams, and hundreds of elite sports organisations worldwide.

I care about clarity, structure, and making sure the people who use what I build can actually do their jobs better because of it.
