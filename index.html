A bit about me

Your photo here

I'm Alex — a product designer based in Dublin with 5 years of experience shipping digital products.

I believe good design is invisible. It gets out of the way and lets people do what they came to do. I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of complexity and simplicity — taking dense information and making it feel effortless.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me cycling along the coast, collecting vinyl, or debating typefaces with anyone who'll listen.
